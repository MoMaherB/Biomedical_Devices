About the Project

The idea for the **Biomedical Company and Devices Application** stemmed from my personal interest in the healthcare industry and a passion for technology. I’ve always been intrigued by how modern medical devices contribute to saving lives, and this inspired me to create a platform that helps healthcare providers make informed decisions about critical medical equipment. The need for transparency and ease of access to information in the medical field is crucial, which is what drives this project.

This project is part of my **Portfolio Project for Holberton School**, and it showcases the culmination of skills learned throughout the program. The timeline for this project spanned over several months, involving stages like planning, designing the database, developing the front-end, and integrating the back-end logic. The goal was not just to build a functional app but to also make it visually appealing and user-friendly. Learn more about Holberton School’s programs here.
